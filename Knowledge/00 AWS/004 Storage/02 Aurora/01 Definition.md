# Pros & Cons
---

* Pros
	* Higher Avaliability & Read Scaling

		* Aurora is **HA native** and the failover is **instantaneous**
	* Higher Performance
		* **3x performance** improvement than PostgreSQL on RDS
		* **5x performance** improvement than MySQL on RDS
